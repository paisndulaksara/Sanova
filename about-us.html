Vision

Solidro International envisions becoming the benchmark for excellence and innovation in the HVAC industry, both in Sri Lanka and internationally. Our vision is to be the go-to partner for clients seeking unparalleled climate control solutions, characterized by advanced technology, reliability, and environmental consciousness. We aspire to continually expand our product portfolio, staying at the forefront of industry trends and emerging technologies. With a focus on sustainable practices, visionary leadership, and a commitment to social responsibility, we aim to contribute positively to the communities we serve. Solidro International's vision is to be the pioneer with cutting-edge HVAC solutions that enhance the quality of life and set new standards for the industry.

Our Mission

At Solidro International (Pvt) Ltd., our mission is to be the unrivaled leader in delivering state-of-the-art HVAC systems, fire detection and protection, and hydronic solutions for commercial, industrial, and domestic applications in Sri Lanka. We are committed to providing our clients with cutting-edge products sourced from renowned global brands, ensuring the highest standards of quality, performance, and innovation. Our dedicated team strives to exceed customer expectations by offering tailor-made solutions, fostering sustainability, and promoting a culture of excellence. Through our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and technological advancement, we aim to create environments that prioritize comfort, safety, and efficiency.
